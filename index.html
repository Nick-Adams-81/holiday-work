Intro

This site is dedicated to the fans of the most influential grunge rock band the Seattle area ever produced. Their style and influence have shaped the landscape of rock music for generations. There run, although to short lived, will be felt for years to come. If you are a fan of Nirvana, or if your just a fan of rock music, enjoy the site!

Band History

Nirvana was a grunge rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington in 1987. It was founded by lead singer and Guitarist Kurt Cobain and Bassist Krist Novoselic, the longest lasting and best known Drummer being Dave Grohl, who joined in 1990. Though the band dissolved in 1994 after the death of Cobain, their music maintains a popular following and continues to influence modern rock culture.
