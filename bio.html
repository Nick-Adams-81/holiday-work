Band Bios

Kurt Cobain

Kurt Donald Cobain(February 20,1967-April 5,1994), he was a singer, songwriter, and musician, but he is best known for being the guitarist for Nirvana. Regarded as a generation x icon, he is considered one of the most iconic and influential rock musicians in the history of alternative music.

Krist novoselic

Krist Anthony Novoselic(born May 16, 1965) is a musiciancand political activist, he is best known as the bassist and founding member of Nirvana.

Dave Grohl

David Eric Grohl(born January 14,1969) is the founder, lead vocalist,rhythm guitarist and primary songwriter for the rock band foo Fighters since 1994, he is the best known as longest lasting drummer for Nirvana from late 1990 to early 1994, when Nirvana disbanded following the death of Cobain.
